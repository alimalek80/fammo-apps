## üìä How It Works

### For Pet Owners

1. **Sign Up & Login**
   - Create account with email and password
   - Optional biometric authentication

2. **Add Your Pets**
   - Input basic information
   - Add photos
   - Specify health conditions and preferences
   - Set dietary information

3. **Get AI Recommendations**
   - Generate personalized nutrition plans
   - Receive health reports and recommendations
   - Track your pet's wellness journey

4. **Find Veterinary Care**
   - Browse nearby clinics
   - View clinic details and working hours
   - Contact clinics directly via phone, email, or website

5. **Manage & Track**
   - View AI history for all your pets
   - Update pet information as needed
   - Monitor health and nutrition over time

### For Veterinary Clinics

1. **Register Your Clinic**
   - Provide basic clinic information
   - Add contact details and location
   - Set working hours
   - Upload clinic logo

2. **Complete Profile**
   - Add veterinarian credentials
   - Specify specializations
   - Enter bio and services

3. **Get Discovered**
   - Appear in clinic search results
   - Get found by nearby pet owners
   - Receive referrals through the app

4. **Manage Your Presence**
   - Update information anytime
   - Track approval status
   - Monitor clinic visibility
