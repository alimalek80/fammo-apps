This is the first public release of FAMMO - your AI-powered pet care companion!

### Added

#### Pet Management
- Add, edit, and delete pet profiles (dogs and cats)
- Detailed pet information including breed, age, weight, and activity level
- Photo gallery for each pet
- Track health conditions and food allergies
- Support for multiple pets per account

#### AI-Powered Nutrition Plans
- Personalized meal recommendations based on pet profile
- Feeding schedules with portion sizes
- Complete nutritional breakdown
- Safety notes and dietary warnings
- History of generated nutrition plans

#### AI Health Reports
- Comprehensive health assessments
- Personalized care recommendations
- Evidence-based wellness suggestions
- Health history tracking

#### Veterinary Clinic Features
- Clinic discovery with search and filters
- Location-based nearby clinic search
- Detailed clinic profiles with:
  - Contact information (phone, email, website, social media)
  - Working hours
  - Veterinarian credentials
  - Specializations
- EOI (Eyes on Initiative) partner identification

#### Clinic Registration (For Veterinarians)
- Multi-step registration process
- Profile management
- Working hours configuration
- Logo upload
- Referral code support
- Email confirmation and admin approval workflow

#### User Experience
- Multi-language support (English, Turkish, Finnish, Dutch)
- Biometric authentication (fingerprint/Face ID)
- Modern, intuitive UI design
- Dark and light mode support
- Secure data storage

#### Security
- HTTPS encryption for all data transfer
- Secure local storage
- Privacy-focused data handling
- Account deletion support

### Technical Details
- Flutter 3.10+ framework
- Material Design 3
- Android 5.0+ support
- iOS support (coming soon)
